FAQ

Questions before you begin.

Can I track more than one hobby?

Yes. The Free MVP supports three hobbies. Unlimited hobbies, deeper history, and richer reviews are planned for Pro.

Is HobbyTrack only for creative hobbies?

No. It works well for music, crafts, fitness, reading, languages, gardening, and personal projects.

Does it send reminders?

Not yet. The MVP focuses on tracking, local persistence, and simple weekly progress first.

Can I export my progress?

Export is planned as part of Pro after the free MVP foundation is in place.

Will my hobby notes be private?

Yes. HobbyTrack is private by default, and your personal logs are never public unless you share them.
